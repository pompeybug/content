We know Portsmouth’s quietway cycle routes get mixed reviews—and now’s _your_ chance to help shape their future.

Join us online on [Thursday 19 June, 19:30–21:00](https://fb.me/e/7DWIhVaNm), for a special event where Portsmouth City Council will share details about the quietways and hear directly from you.

Whether you use the quietways regularly, avoid them, or have ideas for improvement, **your voice matters**. Let’s make sure these routes work for everyone.

* Share your experiences
* Ask questions directly to the council
* Help improve cycling in our city

**[Register here to attend](https://fb.me/e/7DWIhVaNm)**

If you've not seen the quieter routes here's our videos of the:
* [Pink route](https://youtu.be/COGeIJ4wdy0?si=qxeoaTMdtIJl_jcH) (Fratton Park to Pyramids), and
* [Black route](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jJ4CmKdC_KQ) route (Cosham to Fratton Station).

Let’s get a strong turnout and show how much our community cares about safer, smarter streets.
